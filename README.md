## What is VEKA Network?

VEKA Network is a **Survival Multiplayer (SMP)** server designed for players who love vanilla-style gameplay with quality-of-life improvements. Whether you're building solo, trading with friends, or exploring with the community — there's something here for you.

**No pay-to-win. No clutter. Just survival.**

---

## Features

### Core Gameplay
- **Survival Multiplayer** — Classic Minecraft survival with a community twist
- **Economy System** — Earn, trade, and spend with a fully integrated dollar economy
- **Land Claims** — Protect your builds with a golden shovel claim system
- **Player Shops** — Set up ChestShops and run your own store
- **Kits & Rewards** — Starter kits, daily rewards, and exclusive rank perks

### Community
- **Active Discord** — Chat in-game and on Discord simultaneously
- **Fair Moderation** — Active staff team with clear rules
- **No Pay-to-Win** — Ranks are earned, not bought
- **New Player Friendly** — Guide system and welcoming community

### Performance
- **Optimized Server** — Running Paper for maximum performance
- **Anti-Grief** — CoreProtect rollback system for peace of mind
- **Anti-Cheat** — Fair play enforced
- **High Uptime** — Reliable hosting on dedicated hardware

## How to Join

1. Open Minecraft **Java Edition** (version 1.21.1)
2. Click **Multiplayer** → **Add Server**
3. Enter the server address: **`play.veka.gg`** *(update when ready)*
4. Click **Join Server** and enjoy!

> **Note:** This server supports both premium and non-premium accounts. Register with `/register <password> <password>` on first join.

---

## Server Rules

We keep rules simple. Breaking them results in warnings, mutes, or bans.

1. **No Griefing** — Don't destroy or steal from other players
2. **No Hacking** — No hacked clients, x-ray, or unfair mods
3. **No Spam** — Don't flood chat or advertise
4. **Be Respectful** — Treat everyone with respect
5. **No Duping** — Don't exploit duplication glitches
6. **English in Chat** — So staff can moderate fairly

| Rank | How to Get | Perks |

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Is this server pay-to-win?**
A: No. All ranks are earned through playtime and community contribution.

**Q: Can I play with friends?**
A: Absolutely! Claim land near each other, form a town, or just play together.

**Q: Is there a Discord?**
A: Yes! Join our Discord for announcements, chat bridge, and community events.

**Q: What version do I need?**
A: Minecraft Java Edition 1.21.1. ViaVersion is installed so 1.20+ clients can also join.

**Q: Are there resets?**
A: We have no planned world resets. Your builds are safe.
